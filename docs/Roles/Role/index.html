type t =
  1. | Washerwoman
    (*

    You start knowing that 1 of 2 players is a particular Townsfolk.

    *)
  2. | Librarian
    (*

    You start knowing that 1 of 2 players is a particular Outsider (or that zero are in play).

    *)
  3. | Investigator
    (*

    You start knowing that 1 of 2 players is a particular Minion.

    *)
  4. | Chef
    (*

    You start knowing how many pairs of Evil players there are.

    *)
  5. | Empath
    (*

    Each Night, learn how many of your two Neighbours are Evil.

    *)
  6. | FortuneTeller
    (*

    Each Night, choose 2 players: you learn if either is a Demon. There is a Good player that registers as a Demon to you (i.e., red herring).

    *)
  7. | Undertaker
    (*

    Each Night, you learn which Role died by execution the previous Day.

    *)
  8. | Monk
    (*

    Each Night, choose a player (not yourself): they are safe from the Demon tonight.

    *)
  9. | Ravenkeeper
    (*

    If you die at Night, you are woken to choose a player: you learn their Role.

    *)
  10. | Virgin
    (*

    The first time you are nominated, if the nominator is a Townsfolk, they are executed immediately.

    *)
  11. | Slayer
    (*

    Once per game, during the Day, publically choose a player: if they are the Demon, they die.

    *)
  12. | Soldier
    (*

    You are safe from the Demon.

    *)
  13. | Mayor
    (*

    If only 3 players live & no execution occurs, your team wins. If you die at Night, another player might die instead.

    *)
  14. | Butler
    (*

    Each Night, choose a player (not yourself): tomorrow, you may only vote if they are voting too.

    *)
  15. | Drunk
    (*

    You do not know you are the drunk, i.e., you think you are a Townsfolk character, but you are not.

    *)
  16. | Recluse
    (*

    You might register as Evil and as a Minion or Demon, even if dead.

    *)
  17. | Saint
    (*

    If you die by execution, your team loses.

    *)
  18. | Poisoner
    (*

    Each Night, choose a player: they are poisoned tonight and tomorrow-day. (This secretly removes/negates their ability.)

    *)
  19. | Spy
    (*

    Each Night, you see the grimoire. You might register as Good and as a Townsfolk or Outsider, even if dead. (The grimoire shows the state-of-the-game.)

    *)
  20. | ScarletWoman
    (*

    If there are 5 or more players alive and the Demon dies, you become the Demon. (Travellers don't count.)

    *)
  21. | Baron
    (*

    There are extra Outsiders in play (+2).

    *)
  22. | Imp
    (*

    Each Night, choose a player: they die. If you kill yourself this way, a Minion becomes the Imp.

    *)
